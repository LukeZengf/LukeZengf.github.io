About Me

I am a junior at Dublin High School, passionate about technology, math, and programming. I have developed my passion for problem-solving through online competitive programming platforms such as USACO, Google's Kickstart and Code Jam, Leetcode, and Codeforces. I am always looking for opportunities to learn and improve my skills, and am also excited about the opportunity to meet and collaborate with other motivated and talented people.

I hope to use my skills to solve problems that have a positive impact on society, and also to contribute meaningfully to the collective knowledge of humanity.
