About Me

Hello! My name is Luke Zeng and I am currently a junior at Dublin High School. I am passionate about technology, mathematics, and programming, and I am eager to find a career where I can apply these interests. Problem-solving is a critical component of all these subjects, and I have had the opportunity to develop these skills through various online competitions such as the USA Computing Olympiad, Google's Kickstart and Code Jam, Leetcode, and Codeforces. With the time and effort I have invested in these activities, I have achieved success. I am now competing in the platinum division of the USACO, the highest division, and I have an elo rating in the top 5% on the Leetcode platform. I have also participated in Capture the Flag (CTF) events, where I achieved a spot in the top 100 US high school teams in a competition with several thousand teams. Furthermore, I have participated in various student-led hackathons, where I received an award for the best educational project.

I am also passionate about STEM within my high school curriculum. I try to challenge myself by taking many relatively advanced courses such as AP Statistics, AP Calculus, AP Chemistry, and AP Computer Science A. I believe that challenging yourself is the best way to improve, which is why I strive to take the most difficult classes available to me in my field of study.

I am a hardworking student with a 4.67 weighted GPA in my sophomore year and a 4.83 in the first semester of my junior year, with a 4.0 unweighted GPA in both. I am always looking for opportunities to learn more about my interests and improve my skills, whether it be through studying, doing algorithmic problems or other forms of work.
